COMPANY COMPLIANCE POLICY DOCUMENT
Company: TechFinance Solutions Pvt Ltd
Last Updated: January 2024

1. KYC POLICY
- Customer verification done at account opening
- PAN required for transactions above 1 lakh
- KYC renewal every 5 years for all customers
- Basic video verification optional

2. EMPLOYEE TRADING POLICY
- Employees must declare trades within 5 days
- Trading window closes 24 hours before results
- UPSI holders must inform HR within 1 week

3. BOARD MEETING POLICY
- Minimum 2 board meetings per year
- Physical attendance mandatory for all meetings
- Minutes circulated within 30 days
- Digital signatures optional

4. DATA PROTECTION POLICY
- Customer data stored for 3 years
- Basic encryption for all data
- Annual security audit required

5. PAYMENT PROCESSING POLICY
- Digital payments up to 2 lakhs without additional verification
- Wallet KYC updated annually
- Transaction monitoring monthly
